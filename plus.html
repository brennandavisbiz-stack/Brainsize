Membership questions

Do I need to take the test first?

Yes, BrainSize+ is built around your IQ Profile, so you take the free test and get matched to a profile before membership unlocks. That's what makes the training tailored instead of generic.

How do I cancel?

Account → Subscription → Cancel. One click, effective at the end of your billing period. No emails, no phone calls, no "are you sure" gauntlet. You keep access until the period ends and your past reports stay yours forever.

Are retests really fresh every time?

Yes. Our question bank holds thousands of calibrated items, so no two of your tests draw the same set. You can't memorize your way to a higher score, which is exactly why the tracking means something.

Is the one-time report included?

Members get the full report on every test they take, included. The one-time report is for people who only want their results once without a membership.
