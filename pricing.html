Pricing questions

Do I have to pay to take the test?

No. The full 30-question test is free, and you'll see your estimated IQ range, percentile band, and profile name at the end at no cost. You only pay if you want the precise score and full breakdown.

Is the $3.99 report a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment for your report, which stays accessible to you. BrainSize+ is the only recurring option, and it's entirely separate and optional.

How do I cancel BrainSize+?

Account → Subscription → Cancel. One click, effective at the end of your billing period — no email, no phone call, no retention maze. You keep access until the period ends, and your past reports remain yours forever.

What's your refund policy?

Full refund on the report within 7 days, no questions asked. For BrainSize+, cancel anytime and you won't be charged again. Full details on our refund policy page.
